Core Components

RWA-Backed NFT Collection

Every CANA NFT represents a real physical product: streetwear & apparel, lifestyle gear, and limited-edition collectibles. When you mint, you get a digital NFT stored in your wallet and receive a physical product delivered to you. Both are tied via a unique QR code, registered on-chain.

Proof of Ownership via QR Code

Each item is embedded with a QR code that links to the NFT's blockchain record, confirms product authenticity and ownership history, enables resale with royalty triggers, and protects uniqueness in secondary markets. This system prevents counterfeits, empowers resale, and keeps value alive across owners.

One-of-One Protocol: Destruction Before Resale

We don't believe in mass replication. We believe in uniqueness. Before reselling any CANA NFT, the original physical product must be destroyed—a video recording is required as proof. This ensures no two people ever own the same physical item, the rarity and integrity of the NFT remain intact, and the incoming buyer receives a newly manufactured version of the same one-of-one item. It's more than resale—it's legacy transfer.
